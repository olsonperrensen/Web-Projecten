The story bedind it

THE STORY BEHIND IT ... Polaris is the result of combining HTML, SASS, JavaScript, and QJ together. The idea had originally started on JUCE, a C++ framework meant for plugin development and standalone apps. But neither of us knew C++, heck even C on a fluent level (sorry Mrs. Beerens) So, in order to still get started with the project, a shift was made. This web-based audio player is meant for a broader audience than its initial concept, since anyone with a tiny computer (not too old) can become brave enough and try it. A rather unusual feature is that Polaris can also track time. We tried as best as we could in order to deliver a solid, final product. PHP was avoided due to lack of time. Still, everything works locally. I suggest you to click on D.E.M.O. and try it for yourself!
