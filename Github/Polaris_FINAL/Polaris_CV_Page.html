MY STORY

Max Iturria is an independent student, based in Belgium. He considers himself to be a creative technologist. He has been around computers for over since he was ~12 and doing so academically since 2020, when he started his studies in Comptuer Science in Sint-Katelijne-Waver, Flanders region. His interests are focused on full stack web development, extending and designing application infrastructure, maintenance, custom integrations and optimizations.
Max has been on exchange in Belgium since 2018 and became part of a Kempen based volunteering group for international student counselling and mutual cultural understanding, along with workshop related programs in 2019. In his spare time, Max likes to test possible combinations of computers and intrinsic human connecting experiences like (but not only tied to) music, psychology...
His other educational and advocacy efforts include learning languages, spirituality, meditation, philosophy and mathematics. Max grew up in Cordoba, Argentina and right before reaching adulthood he decided to move to Belgium alone. Further topics he considers interesting include pedagogical programming, Neuroscience of religion, Neurotechnology, Neuroethics. He is a student enthousiast that enjoys talking in Spanish, English, Dutch, and his broken French and Italian.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION
Previous projects Max has made include having done research on whether fermentation is a temperature-dependent process in relation to the environment it (yeast) grows, finding out how folk music shape different cultures and their sense of community-belonging, among other projects. Inside the Computer Science world, Max held an Aptoide store providing modified binaries for android that reached up to eight millon downloads. He also did reverse engineering of APKs and EXEs when he was around thirteen, modified integer values using Cheat Engine, tried Wireless Penetration tests, among other projects.
