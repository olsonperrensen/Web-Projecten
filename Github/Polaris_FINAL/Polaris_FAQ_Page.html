F.A.Q
Q1

CAN I UPLOAD ANY FILE FORMAT? Currently our application depends on what the modern web browser engines are capable of doing... After all, we did not reinvent the wheel. What does this mean for you? Very simple. Take a look at our table. Is your file supported? Then you can play it.

Q2

WHAT ARE POLARIS'S MINIMUM SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS? 1Ghz Single-Core or comparable CPU, 128MB RAM, 15MB Hard Disk (Browser Cache Available), 16bit Sound Card (unless you just want to see the animations), Windows 7 or later, Chrome v56 / Firefox v71 / Edge v79 / Opera v14 / Safari v11

Q3

WHAT ARE THOSE FLICKERING THINGS HAPPENING ON MY SCREEN??????????????????????? Visualizers or Visualizations are dynamic add-ons to a standard HTML page that thanks to the technology of Javascript, are able to produce images, portrait several different color combinations, patterns, and textures that change based elements of the music being played. Polaris offers tree primary visualization plugins ( Spiral Guy, Hot Balloon Guy, Clown Guy & MilkDrop) allowing you to create different presets. If you’re feeling invincible, you can even attempt to create your own visualization plugin custom wallpaper so you can share it with your friends and acquaintances. We are currently under construction, so maybe in the future you may be able to see many visualization plugins gallery collections, available from of which users have generated those beautiful pictures.

Q4

YOU MENTIONED I CAN SHARE THE RESULT OF A SONG WITH MY FRIEND? TELL ME MORE... This is where the fun comes in. We've just took advantage of what modern browsers can do with the <canvas> element. This amazing feature built-in in every browser allows you, your friends, family, or your favorite company to set as wallpaper something so unique and meaninful only a few handful of people will understand. Got a great idea? Want to try it out with the millions of Polaris users? Get started now. Check out the last visualizer for all the details. Got a great idea for an online service? Put your idea up on our wishlist.

Q5

CAN POLARIS WORK OFFLINE AS WELL? Yes it can! Be sure to have visited at least one time with a working internet connection. Your cached query of the webpage will enable you to select a song and do all the mathematical calculations needed in order to display our wonderful `speak-for-themselves` visualizers.

Q6
